### Last Call: A Message from the Biru Team

*Published on [blog.biru.gg](https://blog.biru.gg)*

---

Every great night at the bar eventually comes to closing time.

Today, we're sharing that **Biru will be winding down operations on [DATE]**. After [DATE], the Biru marketplace, launchpad, staking features, and Beer Point system will no longer be accessible.

This wasn't an easy decision — but we want to be upfront and honest with the community that has made Biru what it is.

---

#### Your NFTs Are Safe — Nothing Changes for Your Assets

**This is the most important thing to know:** Biru is a non-custodial marketplace. We have never held or controlled any of your NFTs or tokens. Everything you own is in your wallet, on the Soneium blockchain, exactly where it's always been.

**You do not need to take any action to protect your assets.**

- Your NFTs remain fully in your wallet
- Smart contracts on Soneium continue to function independently of Biru
- Your Birunoko, Cellar Batch, Meowtopia, and all other NFTs are on-chain and unaffected
- You can view and trade your NFTs on any Soneium-compatible marketplace

- **Beer Points**: The Beer Point system will be discontinued on [DATE + 2 weeks]. Beer Points are off-chain and non-transferable by design, so there is no action needed on your part.

**Q: Do I need to withdraw or move my NFTs?**
A: No. Biru never held your NFTs. They are in your wallet and are not affected by this change.

1. **Immediate asset safety reassurance** (paragraph 2)

3. **Honest reason** (options provided — choose the most truthful one)
