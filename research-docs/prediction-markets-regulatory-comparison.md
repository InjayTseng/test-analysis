## 7. Counterparty Risk & Fund Custody

### Fund Custody Structure

| Platform | Custody Model | Counterparty Risk |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Polymarket** | Non-custodial (user wallet control); USDC.e on Polygon | Low - users control private keys; smart contract risk remains |
| **Kalshi** | Clearinghouse custody (Kalshi Klear LLC) | Very Low - full collateralization; novation eliminates direct counterparty risk |
| **Opinion Labs** | Non-custodial (wallet-based) | Medium - smart contract and oracle risks |

### Settlement Mechanisms

| Platform | Settlement Asset | Process |
|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Polymarket** | USDC.e on Polygon | Automatic on-chain settlement; YES = $1.00, NO = $0.00; instant withdrawal |
| **Kalshi** | USD | Clearinghouse settlement; verified third-party data sources; regulated process |
| **Opinion Labs** | ERC-20 tokens | Oracle-based settlement via optimistic mechanism with dispute resolution |

### Collateralization

| Platform | Model |
|----------|-------|
| **Polymarket** | Each YES/NO pair fully collateralized with USDC |
| **Kalshi** | All trades fully cash collateralized at trade agreement |
| **Opinion Labs** | Bonded data proposals; dispute resolution via token-weighted voting |
