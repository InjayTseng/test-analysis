# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## The Prediction Market Opportunity

The prediction market industry has experienced explosive growth, expanding from ~$9.3B in 2024 trading volume to **$44B+ in 2025** - a 5x increase. The market is projected to reach **$222.5B by 2026** (Piper Sandler) with long-term TAM estimates of **$95.5B by 2035** (47% CAGR).

## The Big Three

| Platform | Founded | HQ | Funding | 2025 Volume | Market Share |
|----------|---------|-----|---------|-------------|--------------|
| **Kalshi** | 2018 | NYC | $1.7B+ | $23.8B | **66%** |
| **Polymarket** | 2020 | NYC | $2.3B+ | $12-15B | **32%** |
| **Opinion Labs** | 2023 | HK | $5M | $4-5B | **~5%** (40% peak) |

## Key Insight: Market Share Reversal

A dramatic shift occurred in late 2025:
- **December 2024:** Polymarket held 95% market share
- **January 2026:** Kalshi now commands 66%, Polymarket 32%
- Sports betting (Robinhood integration) drove Kalshi's surge

## Winner-Take-Most Dynamics

This market exhibits strong network effects:
- Liquidity begets liquidity
- Two platforms may survive long-term
- Regulatory moats matter more than technology
