## 3. Revenue Model & Fee Structure

| Metric | Polymarket | Kalshi | Opinion Labs |
|--------|------------|--------|--------------|
| **Trading Fees (Global)** | 0% (no fees) | 0.07-7% variable | Dynamic taker fees |
| **US Exchange Fees** | 0.01% (planned) | ~0.8-1.2% average | N/A (non-US) |
| **Maker Fees** | 0% | 0% | 0% |
| **Taker Fees** | 0% (global), 0.10% (US) | 7 * p * (1-p) cents | Variable by probability |
| **Deposit/Withdrawal** | Free | Free | Free |
| **Fee Formula** | Flat percentage | Probability-weighted | Peaks at 50% probability |

### Revenue Streams

| Platform | Primary Revenue | Secondary Revenue | Data/Licensing |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Polymarket** | Future token economics | ICE data distribution | Institutional APIs |
| **Kalshi** | Transaction fees ($263.5M in 2025) | Kalshi Trading (market making) | API access |
| **Opinion Labs** | Trading fees ($1-1.74M/week) | Points/token conversion | TBD |

**Key Insight:** Polymarket operates at negative take rate (paying 4% holding rewards, charging no fees), funded entirely by VC. Kalshi generated $263.5M in fee revenue in 2025. Opinion generates $1-1.74M weekly in fees.
