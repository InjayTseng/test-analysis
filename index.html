Explosive Early Traction
Opinion Labs reached $10B in trading volume within 55 days of mainnet launch, briefly capturing 40%+ market share. This represents exceptional product-market fit for an early-stage startup.

Key Differentiators

AI-Powered Oracle (First of its kind)

Decentralized jury of AI models (Claude, ChatGPT, Grok) with Trusted Execution Environment for tamper-resistance. Human reviewers for factual verification.

Macro-Economic Focus

Primary focus on underserved economic markets: interest rates, inflation, employment data. Chainlink integration for institutional-grade data feeds.

Severely Underfunded
Opinion Labs has raised only $5M compared to Kalshi's $1.89B and Polymarket's $2.3B. This 100x+ funding gap is a critical constraint for long-term competition.
